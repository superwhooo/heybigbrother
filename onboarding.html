Our Promise

It’s not that we don’t want to get to know you — we want you to feel safe being yourself, confident that your life is in your control.

But our purpose isn’t to collect your personal life; it’s to help make the world around you a little safer, for you and everyone.

Big Brother’s purpose is insight-driven safe living — using anonymous patterns and community alerts to make your routes, your city, and your choices a little safer every day.
