BB Promise

It’s not that we don’t want to get to know you, we want you to feel safe being yourself, confident that your life is in your control.

Our purpose isn’t to collect your personal life; it’s to help make the world around safer, for you and everyone you love.

Big Brother’s purpose is insight-driven living, using anonymous patterns and community alerts to make your routes, your city, and your choices a little safer every day.

Your Part of the Promise

  • With respect for privacy. You will not try to access anyone else’s information. You can’t. We don’t either.
  • For genuine safety only. False alerts can divert real help from those who need it.
  • With consent. Add Guardians only with their knowledge.
  • With truth. The details you share during an alert help rescuers reach you faster.

What We Promise You in Return

  • We don’t collect or sell your personal data.
  • Your information stays on your device, shared with authorities only when an SOS is triggered and help must reach you.
  • You can delete your account data at any time.
  • Every feature you see exists to keep you safe — nothing more, nothing less.

Glossary

Guardians
Trusted people you name to receive alerts.
Blips
Anonymous presence markers on the map — no identities shown.
Vault
Encrypted space for evidence and important files.
Panic / SOS
Panic alerts Guardians. SOS contacts emergency services.

Guardian setup

Please add at least one Guardian. They will be notified during Panic or SOS events.

Blips intro

You will see anonymous blips of other people who use Big Brother nearby. You cannot identify or interact with them — they are there to reassure you that you are not alone.

Access priming

We will ask for some permissions (location, microphone, notifications) so the app can help when it matters. You can control these anytime in settings.
